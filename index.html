What is Chelesa?

Chelesa are known for their blue color being signatured everywhere.

Chelesa is one of the premier, top class soccer/football teams in the world. Being the team that either has or produced most of if not a lot of the best players in the world.

Not many fans are satifsified with Chelesa at this moment of time (9/9/2023) because they have been "a banter club". They haven't met expectations or found success since their amazing run in the biggest, hardest, and most competive European competition "The Champions League" where they won. From that time on though, they have sold most of their players from that winning squad and have disappointed fans with the play of the new players and management.

Chelesa is currently 12th place, only 2 spaces higher than that last year despite the team playing 4 games in the English Premier League season. The last time chelesa won the League was in the 2016/2017.
